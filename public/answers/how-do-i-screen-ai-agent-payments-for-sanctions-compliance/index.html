How do I screen AI agent payments for sanctions compliance?

Answer: SanctionsAI provides real-time OFAC/SDN screening for every AI agent transaction — checking sender and recipient wallets, payment metadata, and transaction patterns against global sanctions lists before funds move.

The screening pipeline: payment initiation → entity extraction (wallet address, name, jurisdiction) → multi-list screening (OFAC SDN, EU, UN, UK, and 15+ other lists) → risk scoring → allow/block/hold decision. Average latency: under 200ms. False positive rate: <0.1%.
