What is the X402 protocol for AI agent payments?

Answer: X402 is an open protocol for AI agent-to-agent payments over HTTP. SanctionsAI implements the full X402 spec — wallet verification, payment facilitation, and sanctions screening — allowing AI agents to transact autonomously while maintaining compliance.

X402 (HTTP 402 Payment Required reimagined) defines: wallet discovery (where does this agent accept payments?), payment request (what amount, what token, what chain?), payment verification (did the transaction confirm on-chain?), and receipt generation. SanctionsAI adds the compliance layer: screening both parties before payment executes.
