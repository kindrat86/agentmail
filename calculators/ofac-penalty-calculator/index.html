How the penalty formula works

OFAC starts with the statutory maximum per violation (~$368,000 in 2026) and applies adjustments: (1) egregiousness — was the violation willful or reckless? (2) voluntary self-disclosure — did you report it before OFAC found it? (3) compliance program — did you have documented policies? (4) cooperation — did you assist the investigation? (5) prior history — first offense or repeat?
