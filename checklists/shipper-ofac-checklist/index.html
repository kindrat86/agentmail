Shipping & Trade OFAC Compliance Checklist

Sanctions screening controls for shipping, freight, and international trade companies.

How to use: work through each section in order. Don't skip steps — the order matters.

Counterparty screening

  • Screen every shipper, consignee, and notify party
  • Vessel and IMO number screening
  • Port of loading and discharge screening
  • Beneficial owner screening

Cargo screening

  • Commodity classification vs dual-use lists
  • End-use and end-user verification
  • Transshipment risk assessment
  • Denied-party list screening (BIS, OFAC, EU)

Documentation

  • Screening records retained 5 years
  • Audit trail for every clearance decision
  • Officer sign-off on high-risk shipments

Why this checklist exists

This checklist was built for teams handling OFAC screening API. Each item addresses a common failure mode we've seen in real workflows. Skipping a step doesn't save time — it creates rework, disputes, or missed signals downstream.
