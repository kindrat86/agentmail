Refinitiv World-Check pricing breakdown

Refinitiv World-Check (now part of LSEG) is the legacy standard for PEP, sanctions, and adverse media screening. Like most enterprise compliance tools, pricing is not publicly listed and requires contacting sales. Based on industry benchmarks:

  • Core data access: Typically $20,000–$50,000/year for basic sanctions and PEP list access. Pricing scales with data scope — adding adverse media, enhanced due diligence, or regional list coverage increases cost.
  • API integration: Platform access fees of $10,000–$30,000/year on top of data licensing. Implementation is complex and typically requires 2–4 months of integration work.
  • User seats: Additional per-seat costs for compliance analysts using the dashboard interface, typically $2,000–$5,000/seat/year.
  • Total cost: For a mid-size deployment, expect $50,000–$100,000+/year all-in. Enterprise deployments at large banks can exceed $200,000/year.
