ComplyAdvantage pricing breakdown

ComplyAdvantage offers usage-based pricing for their AI-powered sanctions, PEP, and adverse media screening API. While they do not publish a fixed price list, the following is based on their public pricing page and industry reports:

  • Free trial: 14-day sandbox access with test data and API documentation.
  • Starter tier: Typically $200–$500/month for low-volume screening (up to a few thousand checks/month). Includes sanctions and PEP screening with basic risk scoring.
  • Growth tier: $500–$2,000/month for mid-volume. Adds adverse media screening, enhanced due diligence, and priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for high-volume deployments. Includes dedicated account management, custom risk models, and SLA-backed uptime.
  • Hidden costs: ComplyAdvantage does not screen crypto wallet addresses — only names and entities. If your product handles on-chain payments, you will need a separate wallet screening solution, effectively doubling your compliance stack cost.
