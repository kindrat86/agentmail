The Outreach Playbook
  1. Identify — 100 names with audience size, overlap with "dev building autonomous payment agents," contact method, tier.
  2. Research — Consume 3+ pieces of their content. Find where agentmail complements without competing.
  3. First Touch — Personal email/DM. Reference specific content. Offer value first: free API key, co-authored post, or guest appearance.
  4. Follow Up — Russell's rule: 5 touches. Most partnerships close on #3-5. Different angle each time.
  5. Deliver — Over-deliver. Free access, custom integration guide, co-branded content. Make promoting agentmail the easiest yes.
