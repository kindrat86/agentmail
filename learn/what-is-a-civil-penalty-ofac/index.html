What is OFAC Civil Penalty?

An OFAC civil penalty is a monetary fine imposed for a sanctions violation. Penalties can be substantial and are assessed under a strict-liability standard; OFAC weighs aggravating and mitigating factors, including the quality of a party's compliance program and any voluntary self-disclosure.
